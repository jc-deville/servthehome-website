About Us

Straightforward advice. Clean installs.

ServTheHome started with a simple idea: most people don't need a huge IT contract, they just need someone who knows networking to come out, explain the options in plain language, and do the work neatly. That's still how we operate today.

We primarily serve homeowners who want dependable Wi-Fi and a tidy equipment setup, and we take on a limited number of small business projects where the same hands-on approach makes sense.
